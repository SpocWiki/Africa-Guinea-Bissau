## #has_/text_of_/abstract 

> **Bissau** (Portuguese pronunciation: [biËˆsaw]) is the capital and largest city of Guinea-Bissau. 
> As of 2015, it had a population of 492,004. 
> Bissau is located on the Geba River estuary, off the Atlantic Ocean, 
> and is Guinea-Bissau's largest city, major port, its administrative and military center.
>
